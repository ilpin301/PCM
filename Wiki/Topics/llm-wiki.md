# LLM Wiki

An LLM Wiki is a structured knowledge system that separates captured source material from compiled knowledge notes.

## Overview

The system has two layers:

- **Raw sources** (`Raw/Sources/`) — original content preserved as-is.
- **Wiki notes** (`Wiki/`) — focused, reusable knowledge distilled from Raw sources.

This separation keeps context traceable and prevents knowledge from being buried inside large, unstructured notes.
